About Maryville College

Maryville College is a nationally ranked liberal arts college that has been educating students for over 200 years. Founded in 1819, we are one of the oldest colleges in Tennessee and have a rich tradition of academic excellence.

Our college offers a unique approach to education through our innovative curriculum that combines traditional liberal arts with hands-on experiential learning. Students benefit from small class sizes, personalized attention from faculty, and numerous opportunities for research and internships.

Academic Programs

Maryville College offers more than 30 majors and 40 minors across diverse fields including sciences, humanities, arts, and business. Our signature programs include environmental science, music, and business administration.

The college maintains a 12:1 student-to-faculty ratio, ensuring that every student receives individualized attention and mentorship. Our faculty are dedicated teachers and scholars who are committed to student success.

Campus Life

Located at the foothills of the Great Smoky Mountains, our 320-acre campus provides a beautiful setting for learning and personal growth. Students enjoy access to state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, laboratories, athletic facilities, and residence halls.

With over 50 student organizations, NCAA Division III athletics, and numerous cultural events, there's always something happening on campus. Our vibrant community encourages students to explore their interests, develop leadership skills, and form lasting friendships.

Student Success

Maryville College graduates are well-prepared for careers and graduate school. Our alumni network includes successful professionals in medicine, law, education, business, and the arts. The college's career services office provides comprehensive support for internships, job placement, and graduate school applications.

Admission Information

We welcome students from diverse backgrounds who are passionate about learning and making a difference in the world. Our holistic admission process considers academic achievement, extracurricular involvement, and personal qualities.
